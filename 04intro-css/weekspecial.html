Recipe of the Week

Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 2/3 cups all-purpose or whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup salted butter, softened to room tempurature or melted
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Walmart Pickup

Instructions

  1. Prehead oven to 350 degrees F. Line a 12-cup muffin tin with liners. Set aside
  2. Add all ingredients except chocolate chips tp a large bowl and mix until no dry streaks remain. Stir in chocolate chips.
  3. Scoop batter evenly into muffin tin filling muffin cups about 2/3 to 3/4 full.
  4. Bake for 18-25 minutes until the tops spring back lightly to the touch. Remove from the muffin tin and let cool completely on cooling rack.
